Place your reel video here as "reel.mp4".

The VideoPlane component will automatically pick it up.
If this file is absent, the site displays an animated canvas texture as a placeholder.

Recommended specs:
  - Format: MP4 (H.264)
  - Resolution: 1280x720 or 1920x1080
  - Duration: 60-120 seconds (loops automatically)
  - Audio: muted/silent (the video is muted by policy for autoplay)
